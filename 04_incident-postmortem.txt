- deploy went out 4:30, no canary, friday afternoon deploy (we said we'd stop doing this)

what went well
- fast detection, pagerduty worked
- rollback was clean, sofie knew exactly what shipped

what went badly
- no timeout on external call (classic)
- friday 4:30 deploy w no canary
- alerting told us errors were up but took 14 min to find WHY, observability gap on the validation service
